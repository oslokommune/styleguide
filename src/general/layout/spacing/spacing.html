Spacing classes are available in both margin and padding, from none(0) to 7, at all four sides and as horizontal (left|right) and vertical (top|bottom) groups.

none 1 2 3 4 5 6 7

Sizes

none = 0px 1 = 5px 2 = 10px 3 = 15px 4 = 30px 5 = 50px 6 = 75px 7 = 100px

Classes

      .osg-[padding|margin]-{top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-none
      .osg-[padding|margin]-{top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-1
      .osg-[padding|margin]-{top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-2
      .osg-[padding|margin]-{top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-3
      .osg-[padding|margin]-{top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-4
      .osg-[padding|margin]-{top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-5
      .osg-[padding|margin]-{top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-6
      .osg-[padding|margin]-{top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-7

Example

      .osg-margin-none
      .osg-margin-horizontal-6
      .osg-padding-4
