Spacing classes are available in both margin and padding, from none(0) to 7, at all four sides and as horizontal (left|right) and vertical (top|bottom) groups. Additionally each class is available for the breakpoints small (default), medium and large.

none 1 2 3 4 5 6 7

Sizes

none = 0px 1 = 5px 2 = 10px 3 = 15px 4 = 30px 5 = 50px 6 = 75px 7 = 100px

Classes

.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-none{-breakpoint-medium|large}
.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-1{-breakpoint-medium|large}
.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-2{-breakpoint-medium|large}
.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-3{-breakpoint-medium|large}
.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-4{-breakpoint-medium|large}
.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-5{-breakpoint-medium|large}
.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-6{-breakpoint-medium|large}
.osg-[padding|margin]{-top|right|bottom|left|horizontal|vertical}-7{-breakpoint-medium|large}
